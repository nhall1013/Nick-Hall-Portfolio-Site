Project Announcement

Our team management app will allow users to create teams for intramural leagues, youth leagues, etc. within a single app. Users will have the ability to sign up for teams, schedule games, view team stats, view practice schedules and game schedules as well as events such as tournaments, and message other members of the team or coaches. The front-end will have a team page that displays a general overview of the team, team stats, and upcoming events or announcements. The database will store information on each team such as team members, team stats, and schedules. Our app will help intramural teams, youth teams, etc. manage their teams, register for teams, check for team announcements and schedules from one central location. Organizations that would be able to benefit from our app are cities that have recreational sports programs, schools with intramural sports programs, or any other organizational league.
